About

Kitty Cat Cafe has been a life long dream come true for owners Kevin and Sarah Hamish (seen below). These cat addicted siblings have created a restaurant that patrons and their feline companions both LOVE.

Kitty Cat Cafe serves up dishes for humans and cats alike while boasting the top rated indoor cat playground in Maryland.
