About

Kitty Cat Cafe has been a life long dream for owners Kevin and Sarah Hamish. These cat addicted siblings have strived to create a space that restaurant go-ers and their feline companions alike can both enjoy.

Kitty Cat Cafe serves up dishes for human and cat alike while boasting the top rated indoor cat playground in Maryland.
